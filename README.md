## 📱 About the Project

**Psychea** is an innovative Android app designed to support mental wellness through a structured 20-day program. It delivers daily motivational quotes, ToDo challenges, mental health articles, emotional tracking, and relaxing mini-games. After the 20-day cycle, users can restart while keeping their progress, creating a continuous self-improvement loop.

This app was developed as part of the **Zwolnieni z Teorii** (Youth Social Project Olympiad) initiative.

## 💡 Social Problem Addressed

Poor mental health is a growing societal issue affecting people of all ages, genders, and social backgrounds. Rising levels of depression, anxiety, and emotional burnout can lower quality of life and even lead to self-harm or suicide. **Psychea** aims to be an accessible tool for self-care and mental empowerment.

## 🎯 Key Features

- **📅 Daily Motivation** – Unlock one motivational quote every day for 20 days. All unlocked quotes are stored and can be shared on social media.  
- **🧠 Mood Tracker** – Log your daily mood (1–5 scale). Receive helpful advice based on your emotional state. Track mood history and see your average mood over time.  
- **✅ ToDo List** – Get two mental wellness tasks daily. Check off completed tasks and see how your habits affect your mood.  
- **📚 Articles** – Learn about psychology and wellness through categorized content:
  - Knowledge Zone (10 articles)  
  - Curiosities (5 articles)  
  - Exercise Zone (12 articles)  
  - Healthy Habits (7 articles)  
- **📞 Helplines** – Easy access to national mental health support numbers. One-click call functionality included.  
- **🌊 Waves Game** – Tap to release waves on screen. Helps with focus, stress relief, and mindfulness.  
- **🗑️ ClearWorries** – Write down your worries and symbolically let go of them with a tap.  
- **🎧 Playlists** – Access specially curated Spotify playlists: *Szumy*, *Relax*, *Classics*.  
- **📊 Progress Tracking** – Visualize your emotional data and task completion history.  
- **🌐 Multilingual Support** – App automatically adjusts to English or Polish based on system language.
