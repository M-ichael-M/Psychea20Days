# Psychea – 20 Days to Mental Wellness

> Psychea is currently not available on Google Play, but we are planning a relaunch soon.

---

## 📱 About the Project

Psychea is an Android application designed to support mental well-being through a structured 20-day self-improvement program.

The app provides:
- daily motivational quotes,
- mental health tasks,
- mood tracking,
- educational articles,
- relaxation mini-games.

After completing the 20-day cycle, users can restart the program while preserving their progress, creating a continuous self-development loop.

The project was created as part of the **Zwolnieni z Teorii** social initiative.

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Mental health issues are an increasing global challenge affecting people of all ages.

Rising levels of stress, anxiety, and burnout can significantly reduce quality of life and, in severe cases, lead to self-harm or suicide.

Psychea aims to provide an accessible, structured tool for mental self-care and emotional development.

## 🎯 Key Features

### 📅 Daily Motivation
Unlock one motivational quote each day for 20 days. Quotes are stored and can be shared.

### 🧠 Mood Tracker
Track daily mood (1–5 scale), view history, and get personalized suggestions.

### ✅ Task System
Complete two daily mental health tasks to build healthy habits.

### 📚 Articles
Educational content divided into categories:
- Knowledge Zone
- Facts & Curiosities
- Exercise Zone
- Healthy Habits

### 📞 Helplines
Quick access to mental health support numbers with one-tap calling.

### 🌊 Relaxation Game
Interactive wave-based mini-game designed for stress relief and mindfulness.

### 🗑️ Clear Worries
Write down worries and symbolically “release” them.

### 🎧 Playlists
Curated Spotify playlists:
- Noise
- Relax
- Classical

### 📊 Progress Tracking
Visualize mood trends and completed tasks.

### 🌐 Localization
Automatic language switching (PL / EN based on system settings).
